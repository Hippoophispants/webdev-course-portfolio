The Best Movies According to Me

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

  1. The Batman (2022)

    A very dramatic and moody depiction of Batman.


  2. Hacksaw Ridge (2016)

    A true story about a soldier who went to war and saved numerous lives without violence.


  3. A Silent Voice (2016)

    A sad animated movie about regretting past mistakes and making up for them.
